# Error Sound Alerts 🎵

![Error Sound Alerts Icon](icon.jpeg)

**Stop missing errors. Catch bugs the moment they hit your code.**

Does your coding flow ever get interrupted because you didn't notice a syntax error until you tried to run your app? Or have you ever sat waiting for a terminal command only to find it failed minutes ago? 

**Error Sound Alerts** solves this instantly by giving you audible feedback the moment things go wrong. Stay in the zone and fix issues before they even register in your brain.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

- **Instant Code Alerts**: Hear a subtle sound the second a red squiggly (diagnostic error) appears in your file.
- **Terminal Integration**: Automatically alerts you when a terminal command fails (Exit Code ≠ 0).
- **Sound Dashboard**: A dedicated sidebar "Control Center" to manage your library.
- **Personal Sound Library**:
  - **Upload** your own MP3/WAV files.
  - **Preview** sounds with a single click.
  - **Select** your favorite alert style.
- **Ultra-Lean & Smart**: Uses signature-based diffing to ensure you only hear sounds for *new* errors, preventing audio spam.
- **Customizable**: Control volume, cooldowns, and monitoring scope (Active file vs. Workspace).

---

## 🛠️ How It Works

1.  **Activate**: Click the **Speaker Icon** in the Activity Bar to initialize your Audio Engine.
2.  **Choose Your Sound**: Use the sidebar to pick from the default `mallikgalti.mp3` or upload your own.
3.  **Code & Fly**: Just start coding! If you make a mistake or a terminal command crashes, you'll hear your alert instantly.
